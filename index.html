What is a Tim Hortons Iced Capp?

An Iced Capp is a frozen coffee beverage made using a signature coffee base blended with cream or milk and ice. The result is a thick, chilled drink with a slightly sweet and bold coffee flavor. Customers can customize it by choosing different milk options or adding flavor shots like vanilla, mocha, or caramel.

This drink also contains caffeine, making it both refreshing and energizing at the same time.

Iced Capp Calories by Size

The calorie count of an Iced Capp depends on the size and ingredients used. On average:

  • Medium: around 330–360 calories

Popular Iced Capp Flavors

While the original Iced Capp is the most popular, there are several variations available:

  • Mocha Iced Capp – a chocolate-flavored version
  • Vanilla Iced Capp – smooth and slightly sweet
  • Caramel Iced Capp – rich and dessert-like taste
  • Oreo-style custom versions (available via customization)

These variations allow customers to enjoy different flavors while keeping the same creamy base.

Ingredients Used

The basic ingredients of an Iced Capp include:

  • Coffee concentrate
  • Cream or milk
  • Sugar or syrup base
  • Ice

Optional toppings like whipped cream can also be added for extra flavor and texture.

Is Iced Capp a Good Choice?

The Iced Capp is a delicious treat, but it is not considered a low-calorie drink. Due to its sugar and fat content, it is best enjoyed occasionally. However, lighter versions and milk-based options can make it a better choice for those watching their calorie intake.

It’s ideal for people who enjoy creamy coffee drinks rather than strong black coffee.
